About Us

Ganesh Chitralaya

Ganesh Chitralaya was established in 2019 and is situated in the beautiful city of Puri, Odisha, India. The shop is owned by Shrabani Patra, who is dedicated to bringing creativity to life through costumes.

Our Objective

Our objective is to make your event or competition experience more creative, enjoyable, and memorable. We aim to provide high-quality, unique, and visually appealing costumes that bring out your character in the best possible way.

What We Offer

At Ganesh Chitralaya, we specialize in creating a wide variety of cosplays for all types of fancy dress competitions. Whether it's for a school event or a professional costume contest, we have the perfect outfit tailored to your needs.
